## creative code toolbox

### episode 02: the tools

>    #creative-code toolbox aims to master the basics of #p5js

- **Grid**: proper layout constraints, for better design. we can't even get started without it.
- **Typography**
- **Database**: import for common formats and services
- **Interactivity and sensors**: from click, drag, to device sensors
- **Integration**:  QR codes, share API, integrated sensor request guidelines
- **SVG**: import, export, manipulate
- **Generative**: the cool stuf of controlled randomness, design augmented by machines
- **Other medias**: raster, audio, video, PDF
